About AfriJournal Online

AfriJournal Online is a Botswana-based digital news platform dedicated to telling stories that matter from local communities to regional and international arenas. Our goal is to give a voice to emerging talents, explore underreported issues, and connect readers to the realities shaping Africa today.

Our Mission

To inform, inspire, and empower readers through factual, balanced, and engaging journalism that promotes social progress, creativity, and youth participation in national development.

Our Vision

To become Botswana’s most trusted and innovative digital media hub a home for credible reporting, in-depth analysis, and creative storytelling.

What We Cover

  • News: Up to date stories on politics, governance, and social issues.
  • Sports: Local and regional coverage highlighting rising athletes and major tournaments.
  • Entertainment: Spotlighting Botswana’s vibrant creative industry, artists, and cultural events.
  • Opinion: Thought provoking editorials and perspectives from emerging journalists and contributors.
  • Multimedia: Visual stories, photo galleries, and video features.

Editorial Team

AfriJournal Online was developed as part of the MULT214 Web Technology I project for the Faculty of Information & Communication Technology at Limkokwing University of Creative Technology.

Our team values integrity, creativity, and the power of storytelling in shaping Botswana’s media future.
